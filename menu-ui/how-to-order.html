Wondering how to place a order?

Well first and foremost make sure you have logged in using the form in the bottom right of the page once you've successfully logged in you can navigate to the menu using the navigation bar at the top, from there u can add what you want to get to the cart. Then its as simple as paying for the item and a email will be sent to you when the item is ready to be picked up! its really that simple!
